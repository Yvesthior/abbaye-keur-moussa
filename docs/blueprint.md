# **App Name**: Keur Moussa Sanctuary

## Core Features:

- Abbey Information Display: Showcase the history, mission, and daily life of the Abbaye Keur Moussa with text, images, and videos in a structured format.
- Product Catalog & Ordering: Browse and purchase products made by the monastic community (e.g., incense, crafts, food items), including basic e-commerce functionality for placing orders.
- News & Event Updates: A dynamic section to publish news, announcements, and upcoming events, allowing the abbey to keep visitors informed.
- Donation & Support Module: Provide a simple, secure interface for users to make financial contributions to support the abbey's activities and upkeep.
- Spiritual Reflection Generator: An AI-powered tool that generates short, contemplative reflections or prayers based on user-selected themes (e.g., peace, gratitude, resilience) or a phrase from monastic teachings.
- Contact & Visit Planner: Detailed contact information, interactive maps for directions, and practical advice for visitors planning a pilgrimage or retreat.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A deep, rich maroon red (#520F12). This color, inspired by the abbey's existing branding, evokes a sense of solemnity, heritage, and majesty, forming a strong foundation for the visual experience.
- Background Color: A soft, almost blush-toned off-white (#F7F2F2). This light and serene background ensures readability and provides a peaceful canvas, subtly harmonizing with the primary red.
- Accent Color: A vibrant, elegant rose-magenta (#CE2A77). This accent color, analogous to the primary, offers a striking yet sophisticated contrast, ideal for highlighting key interactive elements and adding a touch of regal warmth.
- Headlines Font: 'Alegreya' (serif). Its elegant and intellectual design lends an air of distinction and refined antiquity suitable for a sacred institution.
- Body Text Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). Paired with Alegreya, Inter provides modern legibility and a neutral, clear presentation for all textual content, ensuring accessibility and comfort for longer readings.
- Use minimalist, outline-based icons with a touch of classical or spiritual symbolism, maintaining a clean yet evocative aesthetic throughout the site.
- Implement a mobile-first responsive design, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience across all devices, with clear hierarchical organization of content to maintain a 'majestic' uncluttered feel.
- Subtle and graceful animations for navigation and content loading (e.g., soft fades, gentle transitions). Avoid anything distracting or overly flashy, keeping focus on reverence and content.
